Biography

Jonathan Crussell is a principal member of the technical staff at Sandia National Laboratories in Livermore, CA. His current research interests include malware analysis, data science, and the development of analytics that are robust in the presence of adversarially crafted input. In the past, his research has included mobile systems, mobile applications, and creating realistic models of networks and systems through emulation.
